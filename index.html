Guild Rules

PAST CONTINUOUS

We use the PAST CONTINUOUS to talk about an action that was in progress at a specific time in the past.

Structure: Subject + WAS / WERE + Verb-ing

"I was fighting the boss yesterday." (I, He, She, It -> WAS)

"They were expecting me." (You, We, They -> WERE)

Guild Rules

Negative & Question

NEGATIVE (Wasn't / Weren't)

"He wasn't looking when I attacked."

QUESTIONS (Invert Was/Were)

"Were you expecting me?"
